About

About Me

Hello! I'm Reginald M. Almonte, a seasoned software engineer with over 15 years of experience in developing scalable software solutions for web, mobile, and desktop platforms. I specialize in languages like JavaScript, Python, C#, and Java, and have a strong background in software development, debugging, and optimization.

Personal Background

I was born on October 12, 1986, in Quezon City, Philippines. In 2015, I made the exciting move to Japan and have been living in Yokohama ever since. The blend of traditional and modern culture in Japan, as well as the innovative tech scene, has inspired me to continue pushing the boundaries of software development.

Career Journey

My professional journey began after graduating Cum Laude with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from the University of the Philippines in 2009. I started my career as a Technology Consultant at Hewlett Packard Asia Pacific, where I provided data quality support and developed tools for data validation and issue analysis.

In 2011, I transitioned to a role as a Researcher and Software Developer at the Digital Signal Processing Laboratory in Quezon City. There, I led projects focusing on speech recognition, pronunciation evaluation, and app development, which laid the foundation for my expertise in AI and digital signal processing.

Since 2015, I've been working as a Senior Software Engineer at i-BRIDGE Inc. in Yokohama. Here, I have led various projects, including content distribution services, AI and body tracking applications, time management systems, and multi-platform UI development. I take pride in driving innovation and optimizing software development processes to meet business goals.

Hobbies & Interests

Outside of work, I have a passion for Web-App Development, Coding Games, and Automation Tools. I am constantly exploring new technologies and keeping up-to-date with the latest trends in the industry. I also enjoy learning Prompt Engineering, as well as expanding my knowledge in Machine Learning and AI.

Away from the keyboard, I enjoy listening to Music, reading Books, exploring Nature, and tending to my Plants. These hobbies keep me grounded and provide a refreshing balance, while also fueling my creativity and curiosity—qualities that are essential in my work as a software engineer.
